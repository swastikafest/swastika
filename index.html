About SWASTIKA'20

SWASTIKA a platform celebrating every vision that creates innovations.To be Staged first in the year 2019, SWASTIKA is expected to stand as the most spectacular techno-management fest of the region. Rising to a higher stratum of science every year, the fest has chronicled its glory all over the State. Events, workshops and exhibitions hosting a convergence of intelligence and technology have witnessed the footfall of thousands of young minds. #SWASTIKA 2K20 .

WHAT FACTORS DRIVE SWASTIKA 2020?

Science Fiction

SWASTIKA 2020, one of the largest techno-management fests of India, is on an odyssey to stretch out one's imaginations to the far reaches, and extract the technology that one sees in their mind's eye.

Out-of-box

Team SWASTIKA aims at breaking down boundaries and thinking out of the box. After all, science fiction is all about letting your imagination run wild and capturing them into spectacular inventions.

Social Responsibility

With the world moving at a breakneck pace, it is important to ensure that everyone moves together. Team SWASTIKA recognizes the need to uphold our social values and virtues to achieve true development.
